Full-Body Home Workout Split

Repeat this weekly routine for balanced fitness

Day 1: Push Focus

Warm-up (3–5 min)

  • Arm circles
  • Wall push-ups
  • Jumping jacks

Workout

  • Incline Push-ups 3x10-12
  • Regular Push-ups 3x8-10
  • Pike Push-ups 3x6-8
  • Triceps Dips 3x10
  • Wall Plank Hold 3x30s

Day 2: Pull + Core

Warm-up

  • Arm swings
  • Cat-cow stretches

Workout

  • Superman Hold 3x30s
  • Resistance Band Rows 3x12
  • Bicep Curls 3x12
  • Reverse Snow Angels 3x10
  • Bicycle Crunches 3x15/side
  • Plank 3x30s

Day 3: Legs + Glutes

Warm-up

  • High knees
  • Butt kicks

Workout

  • Bodyweight Squats 4x12
  • Wall Sit 3x30s
  • Glute Bridges 3x15
  • Walking Lunges 3x10/leg
  • Calf Raises 3x15
  • Side-Lying Leg Raises 3x10/side

Optional: Active Rest Days (Day 4 & Day 6)

Yoga, light cardio, walking, or stretching

Rest Day (Day 7)

Full rest or light stretching
